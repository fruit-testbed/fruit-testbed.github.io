Project Wrap-Up Meeting

London, Thursday 27th June, 2019

The FRuIT project completes its funded phase at the end of June 2019, after nearly three years of research activity. This wrap-up meeting will review our work, highlighting achievements and outlining future plans.

The meeting will be held at Loughborough University London, in room LDN 1.03. Guidance on travelling to the venue can be found here.
